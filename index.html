Timeline

This template came with a timeline. So, I figured I'd make a timeline.

  • 2004

    Co-found OrangeCoat.com

    We've been designing and developing website since before people thought they needed a website. Now, lots of folks don't need a website. Thanks Facebook.

  • 2011

    Co-found What If Greenville

    We teamed up with Greenville Forward to create a tool to collect ideas to make Greenville better. This little idea grew to ImageinThat.

  • 2013

    Co-found OpenWorks

    We weren't looking to help start a co-work space, but when our landlords left it needed to happen. So we help co-found OpenWorks.

  • 2014

    Co-found 10-4 Good City

    The biggest complaint about What If Greenville was there was no action after the idea. 10-4 Good City was founded to take action.

  • 2014

    Co-found ImagineThat.io

    What If Greenville worked as a great way to learn about what folks in Greenville wanted from their city. Seems like a good idea for other cities, companies and organizations. Imagine That!
